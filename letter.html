Open Letter to the Mayor

Dear :

I am writing this letter to request a revamp of the public drinking fountains within New York City. A great deal of the ones within the city are of outdated design, and are inaccessible to handicapped people. Improving on this would make people feel happier in our outdoor spaces, and would be a great boon for the people of our city.

A few improvements that could be made:

  1. Replacing out dated designs with newer models that contain a bottle filling spout.
  2. Improving designs to be more accessible to people in wheelchairs.
  3. Adding more fountains to public parks that have very few.
